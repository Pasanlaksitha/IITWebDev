GAIA

Overview


Project GAIA, spearheaded by WILD TRAILS - Hotel & Spa, stands as a beacon of wildlife conservation adjacent to the captivating Yala National Park in Sri Lanka. Rooted in the principles of sustainability, our initiative is a multifaceted endeavor that addresses the pressing challenges facing the region's diverse fauna and ecosystems.


Central to Project GAIA is our state-of-the-art Wildlife Vet Clinic, where our dedicated team provides essential medical care and rehabilitation services for the park's wildlife. Beyond medical interventions, we are passionate about fostering harmonious coexistence between humans and wildlife through educational programs and awareness campaigns. These initiatives target school children and youth, aiming to instill a deeper understanding and appreciation for nature and conservation.


In addition to our conservation efforts, Project GAIA actively engages the community through volunteer opportunities, including forest and beach cleanup initiatives. These programs not only contribute to the preservation of the park's natural beauty but also offer enriching experiences for participants, from hands-on involvement in conservation projects to immersive wildlife tours. Explore our website to learn more about how you can join us in making a positive impact through Project GAIA.
